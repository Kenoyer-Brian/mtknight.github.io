Site Purpose

SE Idaho Triangle Weather is a site designed to have up-to-date weather information for Preston, Idaho, Soda Springs, Idaho, and Fish Haven, Idaho— three towns that form the South East Triangle of Idaho. This particular area is rich in outdoor activities from fishing to hiking, all of which are affected by weather. This site will assist visitors in determining the appropriate weather gear for their planned activities. This site will also assist local businesses equip and guide said visitors by providing up to date weather conditions.
